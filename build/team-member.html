Hannah Johnson

Strategist

After graduating from the University of California, Irvine with degrees in Political Science and International Relations, Hannah moved to South Korea to work as an educator and storyteller for the television and radio networks EBS, TBS, and Arirang. She returned to the U.S. to embark on a series of road trips to interview inspiring leaders for the PBS documentary series, Roadtrip Nation. Her most recent passion project has been collaborating with my closest friends on the development of a local nonprofit, Eayikes. She is a Strategist here at GOOD and a master of creative problem solving, ideation and creative expression.
